About CSE

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems, focusing on the theoretical foundations and practical applications of algorithms, computation, and automation. It involves the design and development of both software and hardware, the creation of algorithms, and the analysis of data. Core areas include theoretical computer science, algorithms, programming languages, artificial intelligence, data structures, and systems, which are applied in diverse fields to solve complex problems.
